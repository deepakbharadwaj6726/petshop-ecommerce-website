Printable simple resumes by Canva

Creating your resume is a step into the professional world. With Canva’s printable simple resume templates, it does not take much of your time to build your resume. Do it by having professionally-designed selections to help you with a hassle-free design. You can now focus on the quality of your content and make sure your skills are well-represented. Simple resume templates for students and professionals is also a good starting point.

For no-frills, no-fuss resume designs, there’s no better creative design partner than Canva. The online image editing platform lets you choose from an impressive range of formal, simple resume templates which you can adapt for your professional resume. Begin by picking a template from our collection that matches how you vision your resume to look like. Consider your target job and think about how it should communicate through design. Simple resumes are often minimalistic in nature as it capitalizes on functionality. Hiring managers and recruiters often deal with lots of resumes in a day. Choosing to do a simple resume helps them review and scan through your CV quicker. Readability is one of the strongest suits of this type of resume so choose a design that shows such.

Follow it up by inserting your key information. A basic resume template consists of your contact information, work history, and education. You may also include a summary of skills and accomplishments. Remember to be consistent and concise but do not mistake it for not providing enough context. Cap your design by adding elements using our drag-and-drop feature. Even if simple resumes thrive on minimal designs, it doesn't mean you can't flaunt a bit of character. Accentuate with the use of our fonts collection but don't overdo it. Play with our color palettes or make your own, sticking to neutrals or monochrome. Voila! You are a design away from your next job.
